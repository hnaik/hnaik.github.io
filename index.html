Research

I am currently working under the supervision of Prof. Gyorgy Turan. My primary research area is Deep Learning with a focus on enhancing interpretablity in complex Neural Network architectures. Additionally, I am also interested in Common Sense Reasoning, Learning Theory, Knowledge Representation and Knowledge Graphs.

Previously, I dabbled in Human-Centered Computing and worked with human subjects for understanding contexts based on geometric configuration of groups in small meeting spaces.
